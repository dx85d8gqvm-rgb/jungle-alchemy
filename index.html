Your network is your net worth

The people are the point.

The more life moves onto screens, the rarer it gets to be in a room with people who actually get it. That's what this week is really about — a small group of purposeful, grounded people in the same place, building the kind of real connection that's getting harder to find. Spend a week alongside others striving to be their best and building something that lasts, and it may be one of the better investments you make. The friends you meet here are the kind you stay close with long after you fly home.

Rejuvenation

Move your body, eat ridiculously good food, and flow through daily yoga or mobility. Build genuine strength, energy, and feel alive in your body.

Restoration

Sound baths, breathwork, and plenty of space to genuinely unwind. Rest deep, breathe easy, and come home to yourself. The kind of reset that's hard to come by.

Adventure

Surf warm waves, swim under waterfalls, and say yes to the stuff that gets your heart going. The best stories live just past your comfort zone.

Beau Winn

Former Poker Pro → Yoga & Meditation Teacher

Hey, what's up. I'm Beau.

I spent years grinding from the low stakes all the way to the highest levels of pro poker. I learned how to perform at my best and keep my cool when it counted most.

But the real payoff came when I left my poker career behind for good, realizing it was leaving me unfulfilled, without real community, and yearning for more. I went traveling around the world and stumbled into yoga, meditation, nature, new friends, and a different way of living than spending most of my time in a casino. I've been able to cultivate a deeper sense of peace & purpose, and now I want to share it with the world.

These days I'm all about helping people drop the armor, have a genuinely good time, and connect to something deeper & real: community, nature, and purpose. Jungle Alchemy is the trip I wished someone had invited me on, so I'm putting it on myself.
