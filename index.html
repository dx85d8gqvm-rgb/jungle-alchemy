Your network is your net worth

The people are the point.

The more life moves onto screens, the rarer it gets to be in a room with people who actually get it. That's what this week is really about. A small group of driven, grounded people, off their phones and in the same place, building the kind of real connection that's getting harder to find.

Your network really is your net worth. The people around you shape where you end up, so spend a week alongside others who are striving to be the best version of themselves and looking to build something that lasts. The crew you meet here is the kind you stay close with long after you fly home.

Rejuvenation

Move your body, eat ridiculously good food, and flow through daily yoga or mobility. Build genuine strength, energy, and feel alive in your body.

Restoration

Sound baths, breathwork, and plenty of space to genuinely unwind. Rest deep, breathe easy, and come home to yourself. The kind of reset that's hard to come by.

Adventure

Surf warm waves, swim under waterfalls, and say yes to the stuff that gets your heart going. The best stories live just past your comfort zone.

What we get up to.

A week of play, movement, singing, dancing, laughing, and lounging in a hammock. Experience it all.

Yoga & Movement

Sunrise flows in the open air. All levels, all welcome, zero pressure.

Meditation

Surfing

Sound Journeys

Lie back, close your eyes, and let the bowls and gongs do their thing.

Live Music

Fireside jams, wholesome songs, and the kind of nights that will give you memories for years.

Waterfall Adventures

Trek into the jungle, explore beautiful waterfalls, and swim in hidden pools that will leave you feeling purified.

Freedom & Sovereignty Workshops

Easygoing talks on freedom, owning your life, and living on your own terms. Take what's useful, leave the rest.

Downtime

Nothing's packed too tight. Plenty of room to relax in a hammock, swim in the property's creek, or take time to clear your mind. This is a retreat, not a bootcamp.

Your Host

Beau Winn

Former Poker Pro → Yoga & Meditation Teacher

Beau spent years grinding from the low stakes to the highest levels of pro poker. He learned how to perform at his best and keep his cool when it counted most.

But the real payoff came when he left his career behind for good, realizing it was leaving him feeling unfulfilled, lacking real community, and yearning for more. He stumbled into yoga, meditation, nature, and a deeper sense of peace and purpose.

These days he's all about helping people drop the armor, have a genuinely good time, and connecting to something deeper. Jungle Alchemy is the trip he always wished someone had invited him on, so he's putting it on himself.

Jungle Alchemy is not just any retreat. It's a space where people from all around the world can come and drop into a sacred gathering of authentic community building. As AI grows stronger by the day, strong communities and friendships will be more important than ever. As Beau says, it's hard to have a bad life when your relationships are thriving.
